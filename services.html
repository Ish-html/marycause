Our Services

At Mary, Cause of Our Joy Parish, we offer a variety of spiritual and community services to help you grow in faith and deepen your relationship with God. Explore the following services:

  • Mass Celebrations: Join us for daily, weekly, and special Mass services.
  • Baptism: We offer baptisms for children and adults. Contact the parish office for more information.
  • Marriage Preparation: Pre-marriage counseling and preparation for couples looking to get married in the Church.
  • Confession: Receive the sacrament of reconciliation on scheduled days or by appointment.
  • Funeral Services: Our parish provides funeral rites and services to help you during your time of loss.

For more information or to schedule any of the services listed, please don't hesitate to reach out to us through our contact page or visit the parish office.
